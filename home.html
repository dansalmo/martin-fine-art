Philosophy and vision

It is the core belief of Martin Fine Art Classes that anyone can learn to draw and paint skillfully, given the right instruction. This core belief is what drives the instructional approach for both children and adults and makes Martin Fine Art Classes unique in comparison to other art schools or drop-in arts and crafts programs. Martin Fine Art Classes uses the approach that has been in existence for hundreds of years, the old masters’ approach.
