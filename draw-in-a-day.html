Draw in a Day

Line and Value Drawing One Day Workshop

Line drawing is the basic foundation for any 2 or 3 dimension representational creation. Values in art theory or practice refer to the relative lightness or darkness of an object exposed to light.

Workshop Components:

Learning to see

Fundamental line drawing- You will learn to sketch quickly and see relationships in size, placement, and shape in order to correct the drawing. You will learn a variety of strategies to help you correct and develop your sketch into a final line drawing.

Concentration on Values

Working primarily from still life, students will learn and master the key principles for creating the illusion of three dimensions on a two dimensional surface by learning to apply light, middle, and dark values. Students will work with vine charcoal on charcoal paper to apply the key concepts of three general values and developing the entire range of values in a drawing.
